FAQ
Common questions
Are these documents legally valid? ▾
LegalGen generates professionally structured documents based on established legal standards. For complex situations, we recommend a qualified lawyer review the final document.
Which jurisdictions do you support? ▾
Netherlands, Germany, UK, France, Belgium, Spain, and US (New York & California). More being added.
What is the quality score? ▾
After generating your document, our AI evaluates it against standard legal benchmarks for your jurisdiction — checking completeness, clause coverage, and enforceability. It gives you a % score and star rating so you know how solid your document is.
How do templates work? ▾
Once you generate a document you're happy with, save it as a template. Next time, load the template and just change the party names, dates, and any specifics. Perfect for companies working with many freelancers.
